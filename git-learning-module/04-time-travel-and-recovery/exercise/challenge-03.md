# Challenge 03: Reflog Resurrection

**Scenario:**
Disaster has struck. You were attempting to clean up your local workspace and accidentally executed a hard reset, destroying three days of brilliant, un-pushed code. The commits are gone from the standard log. You need to use Git's ultimate safety net to resurrect your lost work.

**Objectives:**
1. Simulate the disaster by performing a hard reset to an older commit.
2. Use the reference log to locate the hash of your "destroyed" work.
3. Forcefully reset your HEAD pointer back to the resurrected commit hash.
4. Verify that all your code is successfully restored.
