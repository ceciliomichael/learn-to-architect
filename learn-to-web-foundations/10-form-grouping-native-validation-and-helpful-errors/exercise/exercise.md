# Exercise: Form Grouping, Native Validation, and Helpful Errors

## Goal

Apply this module in a small page whose structure and behavior you can inspect in the browser.

## Steps

1. Group three delivery choices with fieldset and legend.
2. Require exactly one radio choice.
3. Add a quantity constrained from 1 through 10.
4. Place persistent help text beside the quantity.
5. Test empty, below-range, above-range, and valid submissions.

## Success check

The result must satisfy every step, remain understandable without visual styling alone, and produce no unexplained browser-console or missing-resource errors.
