# Module 15 Quiz Answers

## Answer 1

A definite loop repeats a known number of times. Example: print numbers 1 through 10, or process 25 tickets.

An indefinite loop repeats an unknown number of times and stops when a condition becomes false. Example: read expenses until the user enters 0, or keep asking for a valid age.
