# Exercises: Numbers, Operators, and Conversions

1. Calculate the number of full boxes and leftover items for 53 items in boxes of 8.
2. Calculate a subtotal from a `float64` price and integer quantity.
3. Convert 90 seconds into whole minutes and remaining seconds.
4. Compare one third as integer division and floating-point division.
5. Explain why displaying two decimal places does not make a float exact money storage.
