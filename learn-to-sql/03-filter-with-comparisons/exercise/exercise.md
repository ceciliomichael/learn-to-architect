# Exercises: Filter Rows with Comparisons

## Exercise 1: One condition

Write and run queries for:

1. Books costing at least 30.
2. Books written by Lena Ortiz.
3. Books that are not in stock.

Select only columns that help verify each answer.

## Exercise 2: Combined conditions

Find books that are:

1. In stock and cost less than 30.
2. Written by Mira Chen or Lena Ortiz.
3. Written by either of those authors and cost at least 25.

Use parentheses for the third request.

## Exercise 3: Range, list, and pattern

1. Find prices from 20 through 30, including both endpoints.
2. Find books published in 2019, 2021, or 2023.
3. Find titles beginning with `P`.
4. Find titles containing the word `Safe`.
